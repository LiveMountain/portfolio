Fun Outdoor Activities in New Mexico

Outdoor Areas around New Mexico:

  • Abiquiu Dam
  • El Rito Campground
  • Sipapu Ski Resort
  • Santa Cruz Lake
  • Pajarito Mountain

Abiquiu Dam:

Abiquiu Dam is a state park that allows visitors to enjoy various activities such as boating, fishing, camping, and swimming.

El Rito Campground:

El Rito Campground is a quiet campground about 10 minutes East of the town of El Rito. Near the camp is a stream great for fishing or relaxing nearby

Sipapu Ski Resort

Sipapu Ski Resort is a small resort perfect for beginning skiiers or snowboarders or just for a quiet ski trip.

Santa Cruz Lake

Santa Cruz Lake is a state park where anglers can fish from the banks or catch trophy trout from small boats or kayaks.

Pajarito Mountain

Pajarito Mountain is a beautiful hiking area in the summer and fall seasons and a great place to ski during the winter season.
